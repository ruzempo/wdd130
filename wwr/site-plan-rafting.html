Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to offer the practice of Rafting in the Pescados river, in Veracruz and through this service to obtain income. We will achieve these objectives through:

  • Images: An online portfolio with exciting photos of Rafting.
  • Offers: Price list and promotions.
  • Contact with customers: Contact details and a Whatsapp chat.
  • Generate Leads: Contact Form and CTA Buttons.
  • Tips: Tips to practice Rafting.
  • FAQS: A list of frecuent questions.

Audience

Our target audience is people from 18 to 50 years old, people with economic possibilities and who enjoy activities in nature. People who want to experience new activities and who like strong emotions. The access to our web site shoul be by desktop, laptop, mobile device.
